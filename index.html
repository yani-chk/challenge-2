Spaghetti Carbonara

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This creamy Italian pasta is made with just a few simple ingredients and tastes absolutely amazing!

Preparation Time

  • Total time: 30 minutes
  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g bacon or pancetta
  • 4 eggs
  • 100g Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • A little bit of ol ive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil a pot of water with salt and cook the spaghetti.
  2. Heat some olive oil in a pan and add the garlic.
  3. Add the bacon and cook it until it gets crispy.
  4. Beat the eggs in a bowl and mix in the cheese.
  5. Drain the pasta but keep a cup of the pasta water.
  6. Turn off the heat and mix the pasta with the bacon.
  7. Pour the egg and cheese mix over the pasta and stir fast.
  8. Add a little pasta water to make it creamy.
  9. Add pepper on top and serve right away. Enjoy!
